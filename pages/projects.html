Signature Verification with VGG16 and ResNet50: Hyperparameter Impact Analysis

June 2024

Paper / Thesis that was written for my IB Extended Essay with a focus on Computer Science which achieved a maximum final result of A. I programmed and compared the VGG16 and ResNet50 Convolutional Neural Network Architectures based on their accuracy with Forged Signature Classification (SigComp2011 Dataset). Implemented transfer learning with TensorFlow and Keras, achieveing an accuracy of 85.91% with ResNet50 and 84.25% with VGG16.

Python TensorFlow Keras
